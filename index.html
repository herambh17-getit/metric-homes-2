The Metric journey

Calibrated for the most important financial decision of your life — with respect at every step.

01

The respectful contact

Within 2 hours of your enquiry, a named manager calls — not a telecaller. The first question isn't a pitch. It's "What matters most to you in this home?"

02

The real site visit

You see the actual construction, not a glossy show flat. You leave with a printed brief: RERA number, material specs, the architect's name, all-in pricing.

03

An honest booking

Every material named in the agreement. The all-in cost in writing. RERA-secured payments tied to milestones. You book knowing exactly what you get, and when.

04

Stay in the loop

One named liaison from booking to keys. Monthly WhatsApp updates. Live milestone tracking. If anything slips, you hear it from us first.

Ghar Din — Home Day

Possession day is a ceremony, not a transaction. The flat is professionally cleaned, the lights work, the taps run. You receive a bound possession kit, a handwritten note from your architect, a key in a stamped pouch, and a 30-day snag-resolution guarantee — in writing.
